# Core Use Cases

## Banditry Escalation In Frontier Counties

Fuse livestock price shocks, drought stress, mobility corridor changes, market
closures, and incident contagion to stabilize before raids escalate.

## Election Tension And Localized Unrest Risk

Use rally density, permit activity, rumor pressure indicators, dispute signals,
mobility anomalies, and service disruptions to build a risk posture map.

## Drought To Displacement To Resource Conflict

Monitor rainfall deficits, NDVI, water-point stress, migration pressure, and
livestock mortality proxies to pre-position relief before conflict.

## Food Security And Market Panic

Track staple price volatility, transport disruptions, supply anomalies, and fuel
pass-through to coordinate response across Treasury, Trade, and Agriculture.

## Urban Crime Driven By Economic Stress

Use inflation stress, informal economy proxies, mobility shifts, and incident
patterns to target non-policing interventions and smarter patrol allocation.

## Cross-Border Insecurity Spillover

Track corridor risk, border incident clusters, market disruptions, and
displacement movement for cross-agency planning.

## Critical Infrastructure Disruption Risk

Monitor roads, rail, port, and power corridors to protect national economic
arteries and coordinate response.

## Public Service Stress As A Precursor

Watch school attendance, clinic load, absenteeism, and stockout proxies to
catch silent instability before violence appears.

## Post-Incident Stabilization

Track aftershock patterns, grievance persistence, and market reopening to reduce
recurrence risk.

## Multi-Hazard Governance

Use the same platform with different signal packs for floods, landslides,
epidemics, and compound shocks.
